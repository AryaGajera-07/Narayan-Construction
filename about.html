Our Story

From a Small Dream to Gujarat's Trusted Builder

Narayan Construction was founded in 1996 by Mr. Atulbhai Gajera — a civil contractor (Labour) with a vision to build structures that last generations. Starting with small residential projects in Vapi, we grew steadily by delivering quality that exceeded expectations.

Over two decades, we have expanded our capabilities to handle large-scale commercial complexes, government infrastructure, and multi-storey residential developments.

Our philosophy is simple: Build right. Build safe. Build on time. Every project — whether a family home or a government building — receives the same dedication and excellence.
