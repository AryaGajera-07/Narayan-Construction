Our Services

Comprehensive construction solutions tailored to your needs — residential, commercial, and beyond

What We Offer

Complete Construction Solutions

Every project is unique. We bring the expertise, resources, and passion to deliver the best results — on time and within budget.

Residential Construction

We build custom bungalows, villas, row houses, apartments, and multi-storey residential complexes. From foundation to finishing, every detail is crafted for comfort, durability, and beauty.

  • Custom home design & construction
  • Multi-storey apartments & housing societies
  • Vastu-compliant layouts available
  • Premium interior finishing & woodwork
  • Turnkey construction services

Commercial Construction

From office towers and retail malls to hotels, warehouses, and industrial sheds — we build commercial spaces that are functional, future-ready, and aesthetically impressive.

  • Office buildings & corporate campuses
  • Retail complexes & shopping malls
  • Hotels, resorts & hospitality structures
  • Industrial sheds & factory buildings
  • Warehouses & logistics centers

Renovation & Interiors

Transform your existing space into something extraordinary. We undertake full and partial renovation, remodelling, interior decoration, and structural retrofitting projects with minimal disruption.

  • Full home & office renovation
  • Structural retrofitting & strengthening
  • Modular kitchen & bathroom remodelling
  • Flooring, ceiling & wall finishing
  • Electrical & plumbing upgrades

Government Projects

Empanelled with state and central government bodies, Narayan Construction has a strong track record in delivering public infrastructure projects on time and within budget.

  • Government housing & affordable housing schemes
  • Public buildings & civic infrastructure
  • Road construction & civil works
  • Drainage & water supply projects
  • Educational & healthcare infrastructure
