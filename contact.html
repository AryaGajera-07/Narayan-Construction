Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to questions our clients ask most often.

How long does a residential construction project take?

Depending on the size and complexity, a standard residential home (1,200–2,000 sq. ft.) typically takes 8–14 months from foundation to handover.

Do you provide end-to-end turnkey services?

Yes! We offer complete turnkey construction — from design, approvals, and construction to interior finishing, plumbing, electrical, and landscaping.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Vapi, Surat, Daman, Silvassa, and the wider South Gujarat region. We also take select projects across Gujarat and India.

How transparent is your pricing?

We provide a fully itemized cost breakdown before any work begins. No hidden charges — ever. Any changes are discussed and agreed upon in writing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Narayan Construction holds all required RERA registrations, government contractor empanellment, and full insurance coverage for all projects.

Do you offer post-construction warranty?

Absolutely. All our projects come with a 1-year defect liability period, and we remain accessible for any structural concerns even after handover.
